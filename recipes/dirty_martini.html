Dirty Martini for two

Ingredients

  • 6 colossal pimento stuffed Spanish Queen olives
  • 3 shots room temperature vodka (Tito's Handmade)
  • 1.5 shots vermouth (Dolin Dry)
  • 1 shot olive brine
  • approximately 8 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place 2 martini glasses and cocktail shaker in freezer, chill for 30 minutes
  2. Skewer 3 olives per skewer, place in freezer in glasses
  3. Combine remaining liquid ingredients in cocktail shaker, shake 3 times
  4. Add ice, shake vigorously for 20 seconds
  5. Pour into chilled glasses over olives, enjoy!
